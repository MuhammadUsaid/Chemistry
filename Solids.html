Solids

Classification of solids

On the basis of arrangement, the solids are classified in two types:-

  1. Crystalline Solids:-

    2. Those solids in which atoms, molecules or ions are arranged in three dimensional repititive units having definite pattern of arrangement are called crystalline solids.

    Example
    • sugar
    • alum
    • salt

  2. Amorphous Solids

    3. Those solids in which atoms, molecules or ions donot arrange with repititive three dimensional unit having no definite shape are called amorphous solids.

    Example
    • rubber
    • plastic
    • glass
